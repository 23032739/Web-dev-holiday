Save our wildlife

Endangered animals face extinction due to habitat loss, climate change, and human activities. They are crucial for maintaining ecosystem balance and biodiversity. Conservation efforts like habitat restoration and anti-poaching are vital to protect them and sustain life on Earth. By supporting these efforts, we help preserve nature's balance for future generations.

List Endangered animals

  • Giant panda
  • Asian Elephant
  • Sea turtle
  • TIger
  • polar bear

Giant Panda - Habitat loss from deforestation and low reproduction rates threaten their survival. Tiger - Poaching and habitat destruction drastically reduce their population. Asian Elephant - Deforestation, human conflict, and poaching for ivory endanger them. Polar Bear - Melting Arctic ice from climate change limits their hunting grounds.
